ARGAN HAIR SERUM

5 Reviews Add your review
Available In Stock

$29/$39

This argan hair treatment hydrates and conditions damaged and dry hair.

Hair will be healthy, shiny and in excellent condition. The serum protects the hair against UV damage, pollution and other environmental elements, hydrates and controls fly-a-way hair and split ends; helps eliminate frizziness. Silicone and Paraben free.

How to use:
Shake bottle. Before shampooing apply serum to treat hair; leave approximately 15 minutes. Add shampoo, and style as usual. As hot oil treatment leave 5-10 minutes under plastic cap or hot towel. Shampoo as needed. Style as usual. Restore shine and revitalize locks.

Ingredients: Formulated with 100% organic argan oil and natural derived ingredients: 100% Pure Organic Argan Oil, Almond Oil, Prickly Pear Oil, Apricot Oil, Vitamin E, vegetable Glycerin, Fragrance (parfum).

Not recommended for oily hair.

Size: 30 ml. and 50 ml.
